Welcome

In North America, there are two species of cranes. The Whooping Crane is the rarest cranes species on earth with about 400 birds in the wild. The Sandhill crane is the most abundant species of crane in the world with a population of more than 650,000 birds. Many people around the globe are inspired by the beauty and grace of these birds but are not sure how to find them. We hope this tool will help you find cranes in your local community and be inspired to protect these important places and species
